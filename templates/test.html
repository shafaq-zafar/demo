If you have multiple DDX accounts that you use for your own personal use, you can group them together to allow for easier toggling between them. With grouped accounts:

  • You will always log in with this as your master user account. You will not be able to log in directly with the other user accounts you add to your group.
  • You can access any of the user accounts that are part of the group in your user drop down menu and choosing to switch to the other account.
